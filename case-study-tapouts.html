The Challenge

Tapouts helps children manage anxiety and big emotions through weekly sessions—but explaining the value of an emotional wellness app to busy parents requires the right messaging.

The challenge: create scroll-stopping content that speaks to parents' pain points while highlighting real results.

The testimonial-driven content strategy has helped Tapouts build trust with parents. The consistent visual style strengthened brand recognition, driving increased traffic to their app.
