Eze Sur Mer

How do you bring a fashion brand to life without knowing anything about eComm? Improvise. We developed the look & feel, Creative Directed the photoshoots, and built the custom Shopify platform.

Client

Eze Sur Mer

Industry

Fashion / eCommerce

Plans

Shopify, Creative Direction, Photography

Result

Successful store launch

The Challenge

Eze Sur Mer needed to launch their first eCommerce presence—fast. They had beautiful product but no platform, no photography direction, and no clear visual system for the web. They needed someone who could figure it all out.

The challenge: build an entire eCommerce operation from scratch while developing the brand's digital visual identity.

The Solution

We took a learn-as-you-go approach. Developed the visual aesthetic that would translate their Riviera-inspired clothing into a cohesive digital experience. Art directed professional photoshoots capturing both lifestyle moments and clean product shots. Then custom-built a Shopify storefront that matched the brand's breezy, premium positioning.

What We Delivered

  • Complete visual direction
  • Photoshoot creative direction
  • Custom Shopify theme
  • Product photography guidance
  • Category & navigation UX
  • Launch strategy

The Takeaway

Sometimes the best projects are the ones where you don't know what you're doing at the start. The willingness to figure it out—combined with a strong foundation in design and branding—can take you further than expertise alone.

This project proved that creative problem-solving and a bias toward action beats waiting until you feel "ready."
